Shop Eco-friendly!

Welcome to our project. Our aim is to provide a tool that allows both consumers, and small business providers, to assess the environmental and ethical impact of the items they are buying and selling. Please feel free to explore the site.
Consumers can use the 'consumer form' to check out the rating of products they find out and about. All you need is the cost, weight, material and source, all things you can easily find on the label.
Businesses can sign in to add and view their products.

  • As strong believers in making the most of our natural resources, recycling plays an important factor in our scoring algorithm.

  • During the pandemic, we know how tough it has been for small businesses and individuals. That's why this project is dedicated to you.

  • We're all in this together, on our one Earth. Let's make sure we use it properly.
