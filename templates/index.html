Start buying eco-friendly today

Shopping sustainably needs to become the norm.

Not heard of ShopGreen? Think "Food Hygiene Ratings" but for product sustainability. By using a simple traffic light system, our tool allows consumers and small business providers to assess the environmental and ethical impact of the items they are buying and selling.

  • As strong believers in making the most of our natural resources, recycling plays an important factor in our scoring algorithm.

  • During this pandemic, we know how tough it has been for small businesses and individuals. That's why this project is dedicated to you.

  • We believe that our tool could save you money, as well as do good for the planet. Isn't that a win-win.

Consumers can use the consumer form to check out the rating of products they find out and about. Everything you need to get started can be found on the label.

Businesses can sign in to view their products and analytics and to add new products to their database. Or, register to join the growing number of small businesses who use ShopGreen to help guide their brands sustainability, manage their data and finances and improve their brand name.
