Add Your Products to Your ShopGreen List

Please enter the origin country of your item of clothing, its cost and weight and the two majority materials it is made of, with their respective percentages. If it is only made of one material, enter 0 for the percentage of material 2. We will then calculate how environmentally and ethically friendly your item is based on these factors. An impact value of the clothing on the environment will then be displayed next to your item in your list. A colour will also be displayed, which indicates the impact per kg of material, so you may compare differnet garments in your shop.


Please be aware, the final colour coding may not be a complete representation of the items impact on people or the environment. The production and transport of clothing is a complex operation with many steps and unknowns.
